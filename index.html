What is SilverTech?

HealthTech for seniors.

By 2030, one in four in Singapore will be over the age of 65. This rapidly growing age group disproportionately suffers from numerous costly chronic diseases. Historically, the healthcare experience for seniors has often been complex and opaque, resulting in unnecessary care costs and poor patient outcomes. Consumer-centric digital health tools are beginning to change that.
